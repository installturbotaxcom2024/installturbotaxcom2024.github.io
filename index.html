    1. Update Not Installing: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the update fails, close TurboTax and restart your computer before trying again.

    1. TurboTax Installation Error: If TurboTax fails to install, ensure your system meets the minimum requirements, disable any antivirus software, and retry.

    3. TurboTax Update Error: TurboTax may fail to update due to firewalls or system permissions. Disable your firewall temporarily, or run the software as an administrator.
    4. Activation Issues: If the TurboTax activation code does not work, double-check the characters. If the issue persists, contact TurboTax customer support.
